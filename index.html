Prep for Success

To get the most out of every 60-minute Zoom session, please ensure the student is ready with the following:

  • Digital Ready: A stable internet connection and a device with a working camera/mic.
  • The Essentials: Relevant textbooks, workbooks, and a notebook with pencils/pens.
  • Current Assignments: Any specific homework or digital files you'd like to screen-share.
  • Workspace: A quiet, distraction-free area to help the student focus.
